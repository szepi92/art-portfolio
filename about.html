ABOUT

Réka Szepesvári is a Canadian artist born and raised in Hungary. Réka's primary interest is exploring her identity. More specifically, she is drawn to the concept of acquiring and losing language, culture, identity, and self as a result of cultural displacement.

This site showcases both current and earlier works dating back to 2010. To sneak a peak into what goes on in Réka's head and in her studio, take a look at her blog and follow her Facebook page.

Réka is also passionate about curating and teaching art. She is currently the Director of the Artery Gallery at the University of Waterloo and has worked as the Educational Administrator at Partage Art Gallery.
